About Senior Citizen

In India, people who have attained the age of sixtyfive or above are usually referred to as senior citizens. Every year, World Senior Citizen Day is celebrated on August 21. This year, as well, the special day is being celebrated on the same date. Every year, World Senior Citizen Day is celebrated with a lot of pomp and grandeur.
